League Rules

1. Fair Play

All players must compete with integrity and sportsmanship. Cheating, exploiting game bugs, or unsportsmanlike behavior is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification.

2. Race Regulations

Each race will follow standard F1 regulations, including the use of official F1 tracks. Players must adhere to pit stop and tire change rules.

3. Qualifying and Race Structure

Each race weekend will consist of a qualifying session followed by the race. Points will be awarded based on final race standings, following the official F1 point system.

4. Communication

Players are required to communicate via the league's official Discord server. Coordinating race times and updates will be done through this platform.

5. Penalties

Track cutting, intentional collisions, or any form of unsportsmanlike conduct will result in penalties ranging from time penalties to race bans.
